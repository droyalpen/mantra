About Attorneys

Attorneys are graduate from law school and they are licensed to practice law. Attorneys can and do perform many of the same duties as lawyers such as providing advice, drafting legal documents, and negotiating deals.

Attorneys can represent clients in criminal and civil trials, including personal injury,medical malpractice, and product liability. They handle other aspect of a case, such as conducting legal research, preparing legal briefs, and interviewing witnesses. They are responsible for ensuring that their clients'legal rights are protected,their interests are represented to the best of their ability, and that they are treated fairly by the legal system.
