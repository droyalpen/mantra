Have A Questions?

1 What areas of law does your firm specialize in?
We offer legal services across various practice areas including Intellectual Property, Property & Real Estate, Litigation & Criminal Defence, Debt Recovery & Insolvency, Insurance Law, International Trade, Maritime & Transportation Law, and Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR).
2 Do you represent both individuals and corporate clients?
Yes, we represent a diverse clientele including private individuals, startups, SMEs, multinational corporations, and government agencies.
3 How do I register my trademark or patent in Nigeria?
Our team will guide you through the entire process—from conducting a preliminary search to ensure availability, to filing and obtaining your trademark or patent certificate from the appropriate regulatory body.
4 Can you help with debt recovery from a company or individual?
Absolutely. We specialize in debt recovery and can pursue repayment through court proceedings, asset attachment, or company winding-up actions, depending on the circumstances.
5 Do you offer property management services?
Yes, we provide property management and legal agency services for landowners, including lease administration, tenancy agreements, and compliance with relevant real estate laws.
